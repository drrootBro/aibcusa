About AIBC International LLC

AIBC International LLC (RootBro Software) is an independent software studio based in Ithaca, USA. We build practical, lightweight, and privacy-first AI tools for knowledge workers, creators, and students.

Our philosophy is simple: AI should make life easier — not more complicated. We design tools that are stable, minimal, lifetime-licensed, and powered by user-controlled API keys (BYOK).

Our founder, Mark Cui, builds software that solves real personal problems: writing workflows, AI knowledge organizing, language learning, Excel automation, TCM knowledge systems, and creator productivity tools.
