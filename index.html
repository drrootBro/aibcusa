About AIBC International LLC

AIBC International LLC (operating under the RootBro Software brand) is an independent software studio based in Ithaca, USA. We build practical, lightweight, and privacy-first AI tools for knowledge workers, creators, students, and professionals around the world.

Our philosophy is simple: AI should make work faster without increasing complexity or sacrificing control. We design tools that are affordable, transparent, lifetime-licensed, and powered by your own API keys (BYOK).

Our founder, Mark Cui, focuses on building tools that solve real, personal pain points: writing workflows, knowledge management, spreadsheet productivity, bilingual Chinese learning tools, and AI-enhanced natural health frameworks. These ideas are unified by a single mission: help people think better, work faster, and learn more effectively using human-centered AI.

AIBC is developing a connected ecosystem of AI productivity tools across Word, Excel, Google Workspace, Chrome, education apps, and creator utilities. Each product shares the same principles: minimal setup, no lock-in, privacy-first, and practical usefulness.

We believe the future of AI belongs not to large monolithic platforms, but to small, flexible tools that empower individuals. AIBC is building that future.
