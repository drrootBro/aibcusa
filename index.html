About AIBC International LLC

AIBC International LLC (RootBro Software) is an independent software based in Ithaca, New York , USA. We build practical, lightweight, and privacy-first AI tools for professionals, students, and creators.

Our philosophy: AI should make life easier — not more complicated. We focus on stable, minimal, lifetime-licensed tools powered by user-controlled API keys (BYOK).

We creates tools that solve real personal problems: writing automation, AI knowledge management, bilingual learning, Excel productivity, TCM knowledge indexing, and audio/video automation.
